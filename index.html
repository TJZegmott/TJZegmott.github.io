University of Kent

I am currently working on my PhD research at the University of Kent. My research interests are focused on asteroids and their physical characterisation, specifically the Yarkovsky-Oâ€™Keefe-Radzievskii-Paddack (YORP) effect. The YORP effect is a non-gravitational rotational torque that alters the spin-state of asteroids, it is caused by both incident Solar pressure and the recoil from emission of thermal photons. By using lightcurve inversion and other techniques, it is possible to produce shape models of asteroids and also to learn about their spin-state, which helps us in the search for YORP induced changes in rotation.
